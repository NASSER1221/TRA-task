Language of the Ancient Rune:
The runes glowed softly, written in Eldraeth—a lost tongue spoken by star-born sages. 
Each symbol wove past, present, and future into a living tapestry, its meaning shifting 
with the reader’s heart. Deciphering Eldraeth revealed hidden truths but demanded 
sacrifice, for knowledge came at the price of forgotten innocence.

Mythical Creature Guarding the Highest Peak:
At the summit, the Sky Seraph waited—a majestic dragon with feathers like molten silver and 
eyes burning with celestial fire. Its roar stirred storms, its wings shaped clouds. Fiercely 
protective, the Seraph challenged all who sought passage, guarding secrets of the skies and the 
fate of those daring enough to climb..
